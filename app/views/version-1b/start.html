Apply to adopt a child placed in your care

You can apply to adopt a child placed in your care through:

  • placement by consent
  • a placement order

You can only apply if the child:

  • has lived with you for at least 10 weeks in England or Wales
  • is under 18 at the time you apply
  • has never been married or in a civil partnership

How to apply

It costs £170 to apply.

You can apply to adopt 1 or more child in your care in the same application. It will be £170 for each application, not for each child.

You'll need to provide:

  • the child's details, including when they started living with you
  • information about all applicants, including copies of birth certifcates, marriage certificates if relevant, and xxx
  • details of your local authority or voluntary adoption agency
  • your credit or debit card details
  • DBS certificates for all applicants
Each applicant's DBS certificate must have been issued in the past 3 months. Request a new DBS certifcate if any are older.
