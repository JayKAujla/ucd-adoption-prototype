Apply to adopt a child placed in your care

You can apply to adopt a child who's in your care following a court placement order.

They must have lived with you permanently for at least 10 weeks before the court will look at your application.

You can still fill in and save your application form before this time, but cannot submit until the 10 week period has passed.

Before you start

Your social worker can provide some of the information you need to complete your application.

You'll need the child's:

  • full birth certificate (this includes details of the child's birth parents)
  • placement order details

You'll also need to post the child's full birth certificate to the family court reviewing your application.

Information you give about yourselves and the child you want to adopt will only be seen by the court and social workers.

You'll also be asked about the child's:

  • birth parents, such as name, address and occupation
  • past court proceedings
  • past court proceedings for siblings or half-siblings

Other things you might need to apply

You'll need to upload each applicant's:

  • passport photo page
  • driving licence

If relevant, you'll also need to upload:

  • your marriage or civil partnership certificate, in English or Welsh
  • your spouse or civil partner's death certificate
  • a decree absolute of divorce or decree of nullity of your marriage
  • a dissolution order or nullity order of your civil partnership
  • a decree of judicial separation
