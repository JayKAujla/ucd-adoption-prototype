Overview

You can only apply to adopt a child placed in your care if the child:

  • has a court placement order
  • has lived with you for at least 10 weeks
  • is under 18 at the time you apply
  • has never been married or in a civil partnership

You can only apply if you, or you and your partner if you both want to adopt:

  • have lived in the UK for at least 12 months
  • are 21 or older

You or your partner must also have a fixed and permanent home in the UK, Channel Islands or the Isle of Man.
