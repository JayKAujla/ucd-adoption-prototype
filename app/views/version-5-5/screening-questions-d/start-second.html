Overview

To be adopted, a child must:

  • be under 18 at the time you apply
  • not be married or in a civil partnership

You can only apply if you, or you and your partner, if relevant:

  • have lived in the UK for at least 12 months
  • are 21 or older

You or your partner must also have a fixed and permanent home in the UK, Channel Islands or the Isle of Man.

There's a £170 application fee, so you'll also need a debit or credit card.

You can apply to adopt 1 or more child in your care in the same application. It will be £170 for each application, not for each child.
