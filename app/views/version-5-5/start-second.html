Adoption eligibility

You can only apply to adopt a child placed in your care if the child:

  • has a court placement order
  • was placed with you at least 10 weeks ago
  • is under 18 at the time you apply
  • is not married or in a civil partnership

You can only apply if you, or you and your partner if you both want to adopt, have lived in the UK for at least 12 months.

You or your partner must also:

  • have a fixed and permanent home in the UK, Channel Islands or the Isle of Man
  • be 21 or older

How to apply

Information about the child

You'll need the child's:

  • full birth certificate
  • placement order details

You'll also be asked about their:

  • birth parents, such as name, address and occupation
  • previous court proceedings
  • siblings or half-siblings
Information you give about the child will only be seen by the court and social workers.

Information about you

You'll need to provide each applicant's:

  • passport photo page
  • driving licence

If relevant, you'll also need to upload:

  • your marriage or civil partnership certificate, in English or Welsh
  • a copy of your UK visa
  • any passport pages that have UK immigration entry stamps

There's a £170 application fee, so you'll also need a debit or credit card.

You can apply to adopt 1 or more child in your care in the same application. It will be £170 for each application, not for each child.
